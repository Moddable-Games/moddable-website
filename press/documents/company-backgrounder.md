# Moddable.Games — Company Backgrounder

## One-line

Open-source tabletop gaming platform — one engine, multiple game families, community-driven.

## What we do

Moddable.Games builds a hex-grid online engine that hosts wildly different tabletop game families from a single codebase. We publish our rules under open-source licenses and let anyone create mods, variants, and entirely new games on our platform.

## Market context

The modern board game market generates $3B+ annually. Catan alone has sold 45 million copies. Moddable.Games targets the intersection of tabletop and digital — players who want the depth of physical board games with the convenience of online play and the creativity of modding culture.

## Competitive positioning

No existing platform combines open-source rules, a multi-game engine, and a community marketplace. Tabletop Simulator is a sandbox (not a platform). Board Game Arena licenses games (not open-source). Chess.com is single-game. Moddable.Games is the first to apply the open-source software model to tabletop game design.
