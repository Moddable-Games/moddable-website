# Nukes — Fact Sheet

## The game

**Nukes** is a deterministic hex-tile strategy game. No dice, no randomness — every outcome is calculable before you commit. Think chess-depth on a modular hex board.

## Specs

- **Players:** 2–3
- **Duration:** 45+ minutes
- **Age:** 12+
- **Components:** 44 hex tiles, 75 tokens
- **Rulebook version:** v0.9.3
- **License:** CC-BY-SA 4.0

## How it plays

1. Lay 44 hex tiles in any pattern — setup takes 60 seconds
2. Each player places 6 tokens on their home tiles
3. Players take turns moving, splitting, and detonating tokens
4. Chain reactions cascade across the board
5. Last player standing wins

## The hook

Nukes runs on standard Catan-sized hex tiles. 45 million Catan owners already have a compatible board at home. Our pitch: "You already own half the game."
